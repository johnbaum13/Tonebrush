How layers work

Sketch layer — pure graphite, dampens splashes
Wash layer — soft watercolor, builds with passes
Accent layer — bold opaque color marks
Sustain pedal (CC64) — drop a paint splash at the cursor
CC7 — layer: <43 sketch · 43–85 wash · >85 accent
CC74 — brush size (also sets splash size)
CC71 — manual hue
CC73 — splash messiness
Steering: white keys turn right · black keys turn left · distance from middle C = sharpness · higher pitch = faster
No MIDI? A–L play notes · ←/→ steer · ↑/↓ speed · Z/X/C switch layer · -/+ brush · F = splash · U = undo · B = pen toggle
